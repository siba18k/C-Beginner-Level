There are 5 types of C# Operators:

Arithmetic: used for computations+

	+ Add
	- Subtract
	* Multiply
	/ Divide
	% Remainder of Division
	++ Increment by 1
	— Decrement by 1

Comparison operators:

	> Greater than
	>= Greater than or equal to
	< Less than
	<= Less than or equal to
	.== Equal
	!= Not equal

Logical operators:

	&& Logical AND
	|| Logical OR
	! Logical NOT

Bitwise operators:

	& Bitwise AND
	| Bitwise OR
